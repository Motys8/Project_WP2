# Project_WP2 : Foundite

The site is to find lost items or report found belongings within a community  

Users can post listings with photos and browse a gallery of items sorted by category to quickly identify what belongs to them  

The administrator has a private space to validate new posts manage members and ensure that recovered items are returned to their owners
